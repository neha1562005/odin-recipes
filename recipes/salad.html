Refreshing Salad

Description:

A scrumptious refreshing salad option for everyone

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 small carrot
  • 1 tomato
  • soya sauce
  • shredded chicken
  • tomato ketchup
  • lemon juice
  • black pepper
  • chilli flakes
  • salt
  • toasted sesame seeds

Steps:

  1. Chop the cucumber, carrot and tomato into small cubes of roughly equal size
  2. Add the shredded chicken to the chopped veggies
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of soya sauce and ketchup
  4. Add chilli flakes, salt, and black pepper to taste
  5. Mix everything and add juice of half a lemon
  6. Taste and adjust any seasoning as needed
  7. Top with toasted sesame seeds and enjoy!
