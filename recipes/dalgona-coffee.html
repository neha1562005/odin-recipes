Dalgona Coffee>

Description:

Cold frothy coffee to enjoy everyday

Ingredients:

  • Instant coffee
  • White sugar
  • Milk of preference

Steps:

  1. Take 2 tablespoons of instant coffee and sugar into a mug
  2. Add about a teaspoon of water to the mixture
  3. Using a regular spoon slowly start whisking the mixture and continue until it becomes creamy and lighter in colour
  4. Now scoop this creamy coffee mixture on top of chilled milk
  5. Optionally, you can drizzle on some caramel sauce
  6. Stir to mix the coffee and milk and enjoy the creamiest mug of coffee!
