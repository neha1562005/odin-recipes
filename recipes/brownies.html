Crinkly Brownies>

Description:

Easiest chewy crinkly brownies for days you have a sweet tooth

Ingredients:

  • Cocoa powder
  • Icing sugar
  • Melted butter
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Eggs
  • AP flour

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix together 1 cup of high quality cocoa powder, 1/4 cup AP flour, 1.5 cups icing sugar and a pinch of salt
  2. Add 2 eggs to the dry mixture and whisk until fully mixed
  3. Add 1/2 cup of melted butter and oil and mix until just mixed
  4. Line a baking tray with parchment paper
  5. Pour the brownie mixture into the tray and tap the tray lightly to distribute the mixture evenly
  6. Bake at 180 degree celsius for about 15 minutes or until done
  7. Cut into squares and enjoy!
